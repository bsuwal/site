Bhushan Suwal

I am an associate researcher at the MGGG Redistricting Lab at Tufts University's Tisch College of Civic Life led by Moon Duchin. My work is broadly on the computational problems surrounding redistricting and US voting rights. More generally, I am interested in algorithmic fairness.

I graduated in 2019 from Tufts University with an undergraduate degree in Computer Science.

I am based in Somerville, MA.
